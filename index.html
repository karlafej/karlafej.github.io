Adding tables into ggplot plots

@karla · Mar 19, 2018 · 2 min read

Sometimes I want to see a table inside a plot in R. Let’s say that a colleague comes to me with a question: How come there are phenotyping data for a knockout strain XY even if it’s not viable? In this case I want to see a plot of homozygous/heterozygous pups alongside with a table of the viability screen outcomes. Thanks to the tableGrob function from the gridExtra package, this is quite easy.
